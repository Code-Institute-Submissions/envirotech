Dublin 12 Bridge Project
Dublin county council, in partnership with Trust Engineering Ltd, have proposed the building of a bridge over water in the Dublin 12 area. This project is expected to begin construction in December 2020. They have carried out an initial environmental assessment; the details of which are detailed below. The initial public consultations will take place on June 18th 2020. You can get notified of of times and locations by signing up below.

Biodiversity (Plants & Animals)

Construction (Unmitigated)
Overall Impact: Moderate
Potential for water quality to be impacted by abutment structure
Potential for animal habitat damage or destruction
Construction (Mitigated)
Overall Impact: Slight
Bridge to be designed without in-stream support to avoid major impact on water quality

Fuelling of machinery to take place 100m from water to avoid pollution
Potential for animal habitat damage or destruction remains
Operation
Overall Impact: Slight
Potential for traffic fuel leakage into water mitigated by bridge design
Potential for permanent animal habitat damage or destruction still remains due to land take

Air Quality and Climate

Construction (Unmitigated)
Overall Impact: Slight
Potential for slight air pollution due to dust
Construction (Mitigated)
Overall Impact: Slight
Dust barriers and PPE (Personel Protective Equipment) used to significantly reduce impacts
Operation
Overall Impact: Slight
Potential for traffic to localise concentration of emissions in that area, however this impact would be insignificant

Noise and Vibration

Construction (Unmitigated)
Overall Impact: Moderate to Significant
Potential for significant increase in noise during construction.

Current Noise: 45-50 dB
During Construction: 65-70 dB
Construction (Mitigated)
Overall Impact: Moderate
Peak construction scheduled for day-time (during working hours)
Operation
Overall Impact: Slight
Potential for dwellings within 10-20m to experience increased traffic noise

Noise barriers could be installed to reduce potential noise pollution (decided by land owners)

Landscape and Visual

Construction (Unmitigated)
Overall Impact: Moderate
Presence of construction machinery in the area
Construction (Mitigated)
Overall Impact: Slight
Visual barriers placed around the site to reduce visual pollution

Site deliveries localized to one area to reduce spread of construction traffic
Operation
Overall Impact: None
No lasting impacts. The area is urban in nature and the project will fit the environment

Population and Human Health

Construction (Unmitigated)
Overall Impact: Slight
Provides construction employment in the area
No amenities near-by will be affected
Traffic diversions in place
Construction (Mitigated)
Overall Impact: Slight
Manned traffic management in place to reduce traffic build-up
Operation
Overall Impact: None
Improved transport links
Cycle lanes and footpaths allow for safe crossing
