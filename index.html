Envirotech

Inform and Protect

Envirotech is a website dedicated to providing communities around the country with up-to-date, easy to digest information on the environmental impacts of projects happening near them. As our country continues to expand and more and more infrastructure is built, it is our duty as citizens to educate ourselves on the impact of these developments so that we can make informed appeals if necessary.

How Does Envirotech Work?

What is a Environmental Assessment?

During the design of any project, an environmental assessment is carried in order to measure the potential environmental impacts that may occur as a result of a project being built. Assessments measure potential impacts both during construction and after the project is complete.

Why Should I Care?

There are potential environmental impacts for any project that is proposed; whether it be a new road or bridge, a new school, a data center, or even a pathway. This infrastructure can have potential impacts on both your life and the life of animals and plants around you. It is your right as a citizen to help make informed contributions in order to protect the environment around you.

What information can I find?

This website will help you to make informed contributions by providing information such as:

  • - The impact of a project on animal habitats
  • - The potential for improved transport routes
  • - Possibility of noise and air pollution
  • - Impacts on water quality
